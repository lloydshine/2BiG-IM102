What is 2BiG?
2BiG

2BiG is a web-based platform that allows customers to place orders for water delivery, manage their account information, and track their deliveries. The system streamlines the ordering process, providing customers with a convenient and efficient way to manage their water needs. It also enables delivery providers to manage their deliveries and ensure timely and accurate service. The water ordering system typically includes features such as a user-friendly interface, order processing, payment processing, delivery tracking, and customer account management.
